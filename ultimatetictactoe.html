HOW TO PLAY

  1. The game is a large 3×3 grid, each cell containing a smaller 3×3 Tic-Tac-Toe board.
  2. Your move determines where your opponent must play next. Play in the top-right cell → opponent plays in the top-right small board.
  3. If the target small board is already won or full, the opponent can play anywhere.
  4. Win a small board by getting 3 in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).
  5. Win the game by winning three small boards in a row on the big grid.
